Caffea Unlimited was created by our founder, Griffin Overmyer, after his passion for coffee and the culture that comes with it made him want to open his own shop. His knowledge from books about espresso, meeting the great companies in person at exhibitions, and continually critiquing his own coffee methods prepared him to open his own shop in downtown Denver, CO. He would like to thank his parents for believing in his wild dream of opening a coffee shop.
